About

3DOX is a tic-tac-toe in 3D, based on three.js and node.js. To play you have to have a someone who will join to your room.

To play use your mouse. To set a sign on the field click LPM, to move a camera hold LPM and slide. If you want to zoom in or zoom out use a scroll button. Enjoy.
